Instructions

Type your message into the box and click Publish to sent it to the sign.
Customize your message by adding tags to change the text color, font, and transitions.

<hold><green><7shadow><speed1><flash>thechillbar</flash>
<rotate><amber><7><speed3><\n>check us out at thechillbar.mit.edu
